Rose

Roses are one of the most popular flowers in the world. They come in a variety of colors and sizes and are often used for their fragrance. To prepare roses, cut the stems at an angle and remove any leaves that will be submerged in water.

Tulip

Tulips are a classic spring flower that come in a variety of colors. They are easy to grow and require little maintenance. To prepare tulips, remove any foliage that is wilted or discolored.
